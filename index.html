About Us

Dignity Partnership (DiP) is a social enterprise that is committed to explore the untapped skills for refugees and asylum seekers. DiP is committed to inspire people who are living in challenging situations to rise above their difficult situations as quoted by Sigmund Freud ‘When inspiration doesn’t come to me, I go halfway to meet it’.


Objectives

  • Addressing enforced idleness and giving opportunity to people to explore personal development while they improve their skills and knowledge for future empowerment / employment.

  • To empower individuals to improve / develop core skills in business/ entrepreneurship, and in doing so supporting them to unlock their individual talents and potential.

  • To provide knowledge and the Irish experience of running small business.

Mission

Our mission is to support people who have or are living in direct provision to upskill/ reskill through our programme. We provide support on entrepreneur skills development, providing mentoring and the Irish experience of running small business in Ireland.


Vision

Empowered people, with a sense of autonomy and better mental wellbeing.


Core Values

  • Empowerment - Increase skills awareness, and confidence building (some people don’t know or underestimate their talent)

  • Dignity - Restoring self-worth and value.

  • Diversity - Celebrating Differences and Embracing Similarities.

  • Inclusion - Community involvement and having a voice
